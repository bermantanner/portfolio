About Me

Bio

I grew up on computers, built three of my own, and spent a lot of time on games as a kid. That curiosity pulled me toward Computer Science. I'm excited by software that can impact millions at once, and I love exploring autonomous vehicles, agentic AI, and (lately) operating systems.

Outside of class, I'm a guitarist/vocalist and songwriter. I spent two years in a Santa Barbara band with 20+ shows, including dates in San Jose and LA (plus a couple bands back in high school). I ran sprints in early high school, then fell for beach volleyball after graduating; now I play with UCSD's Beach Volleyball club and friends. I'm a new but enthusiastic skier. Favorite foods: dim sum (pork & shrimp shumai is the best), sushi, Thai, and Mexican.
